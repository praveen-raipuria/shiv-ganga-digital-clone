About MBA

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a postgraduate program designed to prepare students for leadership roles in business. The curriculum includes core subjects such as finance, marketing, operations, and strategy. Students engage in real-world case studies, group projects, and internships to apply their knowledge practically. The program also emphasizes communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. Graduates often pursue management positions across various industries, driving organizational growth and innovation.
