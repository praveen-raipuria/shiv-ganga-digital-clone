SEO,SMM & SMO

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and SMM (Social Media Marketing) are essential components of digital marketing. SEO focuses on optimizing websites to rank higher in search engine results

SMM involves promoting brands and content on social media platforms to engage and attract audiences.

SMO stands for Social Media Optimization. It involves optimizing social media profiles and content to increase visibility, engagement, and brand awareness on social media platforms.
